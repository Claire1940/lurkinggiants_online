<Section icon={Shield} title="Map Survival and Hiding Strategies">

Surviving a round requires a deep understanding of the environment. The Forest and City maps demand entirely different movement styles and defensive tactics.

### Key Map Environments

- **Forest Map**: Built around natural cover, trees, bushes, and deep caves. Visibility is extremely low, making darkness your primary ally.
- **City Map**: A highly vertical environment featuring buildings, stairwells, rooftops, and tight alleyways. Verticality allows you to break line of sight quickly.

| Location Type | Survival Rating | Best Use Case | Risk Factor |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Caves** | Good | Temporary hiding during active patrols | High risk of being cornered if spotted |
| **Rooftops** | Excellent | Spotting the Giant from a safe distance | Dead ends can trap you without a drop route |
| **Solid Walls** | Great | Rotating around obstacles during a chase | Requires constant camera movement |

<Callout type="info" title="The Safe Rotation Rule">
Never stay in one hiding spot for an entire round. Once a Giant patrols near your area, wait for them to pass, then immediately rotate to a new covered position.
</Callout>
