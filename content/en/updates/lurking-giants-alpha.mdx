<Section icon={Shield} title="Map Layouts and Tactical Hiding Spots">

Lurking Giants Alpha features diverse environments that require different survival strategies. Breaking the Giant's line of sight is your primary defense.

### Map Comparison: Forest vs. City

| Map | Environment Type | Primary Cover | Verticality |
|-----|------------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Forest** | Outdoor Wilderness | Trees, dense bushes, dark caves | Low (mostly uneven terrain) |
| **City** | Urban Ruins | Buildings, alleys, stairwells | High (rooftops and scaffolding) |

### Key Hiding Strategies

- **Caves & Dark Recesses**: Dark areas hide your avatar's silhouette. Stay deep inside to avoid being outlined by external light sources, but always keep an eye on the exit.
- **Rooftops & High Positions**: Climbing elevated structures in the City map gives you a clear line of sight to track the Giant. Always ensure there is a secondary drop-down route in case you are cornered.
- **Solid Obstacle Rotation**: Use large rocks or building corners to keep a physical barrier between you and the Giant. If the Giant circles left, rotate right to stay hidden.

<Callout type="info" title="Hiding Spot Rule">
A hiding spot is only safe until it is checked. Once a Giant passes your lane, prepare to rotate to a new position. Staying in one spot for an entire round is a guaranteed way to get eliminated.
</Callout>
