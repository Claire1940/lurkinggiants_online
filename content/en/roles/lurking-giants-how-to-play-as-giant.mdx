<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Hunting Strategy">

To achieve a flawless victory and trigger the dramatic "Last Man Standing" phase, you must hunt efficiently. Wandering around without a plan allows survivors to stall out the match timer.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The TV Spawn Phase">
    Wait out the television static animation. Use this brief window to scan the immediate surrounding area for any survivors who spawned too close to your starting point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sweep the Perimeter Walls">
    Inexperienced survivors often run along the outer boundaries of the map. Run a quick lap around the outer walls to easily pick off players who are cornered by the map limits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Grouped Targets">
    When the automatic ping system reveals multiple players grouped together, strike them first. Because your attack has no cooldown, you can chain multiple kills in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Handle Rooftop Campers">
    If survivors climb high towers, do not waste minutes slowly walking up the stairs. Wait at the base or use structural geometry to force them to jump down into your grasp.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Note">
If a survivor manages to jump onto your back, they can trigger a flinging glitch. If this happens, quickly crouch or walk against a solid wall to force them off.
</Callout>

</Section>
