### Sprinting and Stamina Controls
Unlike most games that map sprint to the Shift key, this title maps sprinting to the **E** key. Managing this resource prevents you from being caught defenseless in the open.

| Control Action | Key Binding | Optimal Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Sprint | E | Use only when actively pursued or transitioning between high spots |
| Shift Lock | Shift | Toggle off when climbing ladders to prevent camera glitches |
| Lantern | Click/Tap | Keep off unless absolutely necessary to navigate dark indoor areas |
