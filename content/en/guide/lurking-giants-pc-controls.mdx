<Section icon={Target} title="How to Optimize PC Settings for Survival">

To get the most out of your **lurking giants pc controls**, you should optimize your Roblox client settings. High camera sensitivity and proper toggle choices will drastically improve your reaction times.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Shift Lock Switch">
    Press **Esc**, navigate to the Settings tab, and ensure **Shift Lock Switch** is set to **On**. This allows you to lock your cursor and steer your character dynamically with your mouse.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Adjust Camera Sensitivity">
    Set your camera sensitivity between **0.5 and 1.5** depending on your mouse DPI. You need to be able to whip your camera around 180 degrees quickly to check behind you while sprinting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Configure Graphics Settings">
    Lurking Giants is an extremely dark game. Set your graphics level to at least **4** to render shadows and flashlights properly, but keep it low enough to prevent frame drops during intense chases.
  </Step>
</Steps>
