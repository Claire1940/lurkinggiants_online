Survivors have access to a few basic tools to navigate the pitch-black maps of Lurking Giants. Choosing the right light source and understanding map layouts can mean the difference between surviving until dawn or becoming a giant's next meal.

  <Card title="The Flashlight">

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Survival Walkthrough">

To consistently survive matches in Lurking Giants, players must follow a disciplined loop of movement, hiding, and observation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure High Ground Immediately">
    As soon as the match starts, look for staircases, ladders, or elevated platforms. Staying on the ground level makes you an easy target for the giant's sweeping attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Killer's Movement Patterns">
    Observe the monster from a safe distance. Some players controlling the monster may struggle with navigation, while others use advanced tracking to hunt down targets aggressively.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Light and Sound">
    Turn off your flashlight when the monster approaches your sector. Listen closely for heavy thuds, static noises, and breathing to gauge the killer's proximity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Evade and Relocate">
    If spotted, do not run in a straight line. Utilize the map's geometry, slide under tight spaces, or climb to break the monster's line of sight before finding a new hiding spot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
