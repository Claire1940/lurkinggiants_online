| Key / Input | Action | Description |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Movement and Combat Mechanics">

Once you familiarize yourself with the basic inputs, you must learn how to combine them for advanced maneuvers. Giants do not stand still, and relying solely on walking will quickly lead to defeat. You need to chain jumps, dodges, and grapple inputs to scale these massive creatures and reach their weak spots.

| Action Combo | Input Sequence | Tactical Benefit |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Dodge Jump** | Q + Spacebar | Extends the distance of your dodge roll to clear wide sweep attacks. |
| **Grapple Launch** | F + Spacebar | Pulls you toward a giant and launches you upward for aerial strikes. |
| **Slide Recovery** | S + Shift (while falling) | Reduces fall damage and transitions immediately into a run. |

For players who prefer playing on consoles or using a gamepad on PC, the game supports full controller mapping. The layout is optimized to keep your thumbs on the thumbsticks as much as possible during intense fights.

Mobile players also have access to a dedicated touch interface. The on-screen buttons are placed to mimic console controllers, though customization is slightly more limited.

| Touch Element | Action | Optimization Tip |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Virtual Joystick** | Movement | Keep joystick size to medium for faster directional changes. |
| **Right Side Buttons** | Attack & Dodge | Position your right thumb slightly higher to avoid misclicks. |
| **On-Screen Reticle** | Grapple Aiming | Tap the screen directly to lock onto giant limbs quickly. |
