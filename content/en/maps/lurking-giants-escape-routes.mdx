The Forest map is a dark, sprawling outdoor arena filled with natural obstacles. Surviving here requires utilizing the organic terrain to break the Giant's line of sight. Because there are fewer high platforms, horizontal rotation and clever positioning around solid objects are your primary defensive tools.

| Location Type | Defensive Strength | Main Risk | Rotation Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tree Lines** | High concealment | Open gaps between trees | Move diagonally to break sightlines |
| **Caves & Recesses** | Excellent darkness | Single entrance trap | Exit immediately when the Giant approaches |
| **Bushes** | High visual cover | Zero physical collision | Use only for temporary hiding |

<Section icon={Target} title="City Map Verticality and Urban Escapes">

The City map offers a highly structural layout with significant verticality. While rooftops and stairwells provide excellent vantage points to track the Giant's movements, they can easily become deadly traps if you do not plan your exit routes ahead of time.

| Urban Landmark | Tactical Advantage | Danger Factor | Escape Route Options |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rooftops** | Excellent visibility | Dead ends, fall damage | Connecting bridges, drop-down ledges |
| **Stairwells** | Multi-level transitions | Narrow choke points | Double-back maneuvers, window vaults |
| **Alleyways** | Tight corners | Linear sightlines | Sharp turns around building corners |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the High Ground">
    Climb stairs to reach rooftops and gain a clear view of the Giant's patrol routes. Keep away from the very edge to avoid silhouetting yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Two Exits">
    Never stay on a roof that only has one ladder or staircase. Ensure there is a nearby platform, lower ledge, or drop route available.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Corner Breaks">
    If chased on the ground, use building corners to break direct line of sight. Immediately crouch or change elevation once out of view to throw off the pursuer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Vertical Safety">
Giants take longer to navigate tight stairwells and climb structures. Use this physical delay to gain distance and slip into a dark ground-level alley.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Hunter">
    Recognize which Giant is chasing you. Premium Giants like **Guilt** exert massive physical pressure, requiring early and decisive route choices.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Conserve Sprint Stamina">
    Only activate your sprint when the Giant is moving directly toward you. Use normal walking speed to navigate behind obstacles silently.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Break Line of Sight">
