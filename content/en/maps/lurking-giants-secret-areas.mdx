<Section icon={Shield} title="Forest Map Hiding Spots and Dark Recesses">

The Forest map is a massive outdoor environment characterized by dense foliage, rocky cliffs, and uneven ground. Because of the open nature of this map, staying in the middle of a clearing is highly dangerous. Instead, survivors must learn to navigate the edge of the map and utilize natural barriers to interrupt the Giant's line of sight.

Caves and dark recesses serve as excellent temporary hiding zones. However, entering a cave without an exit plan can easily turn it into a dead end if the Giant decides to investigate.

| Location Type | Best Usage | Visual Cover | Trap Danger | Rotation Ease |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Deep Caves** | Temporary concealment | Excellent | High (One entrance) | Low |
| **Tree Lines** | Mid-round rotation | Good | Low | High |
| **Dark Recesses** | Evading active chases | Excellent | Medium | Medium |
| **Rock Formations** | Looping the Giant | Moderate | Low | Excellent |

**Key Forest Survival Tactics:**
- Use the natural curvature of the land to block the Giant's direct vision while moving between zones.
- Treat caves as short-term staging areas rather than permanent shelters.
- Listen carefully for the heavy footsteps of approaching Giants like Locust or Guilt before exiting dark areas.
- Avoid grouping up with other survivors in a single cave to minimize team wipes.

<Callout type="warning" title="Cave Warning">
If you see the Giant heading directly toward your cave entrance, do not wait inside. Attempt to slip past them early using the outer edges of the opening before you are completely cornered.
</Callout>
