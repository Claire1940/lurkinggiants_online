<Section icon={Map} title="Strategic Map Navigation & Hiding Spots">

The map is designed with several distinct landmarks that offer varying degrees of safety. For a solo player, knowing which areas provide hard cover versus soft cover is vital. The graveyard and the towers are the most prominent features, but the smaller cabins and caves often provide better long-term survival prospects.

| Location | Safety Level | Features | Best Strategy |
|----------|--------------|----------|---------------|
| **The Towers** | Moderate | High visibility, limited exits. | Use for scouting, then leave quickly. |
| **The Graveyard** | Low | Open space, frequent giant spawns. | Avoid during peak night hours. |
| **The Cabin** | High | Interior walls, multiple rooms. | Best for hiding when the giant is near. |
| **The Cave** | Moderate | Deep cover, limited visibility. | Good for losing a giant's line of sight. |
| **The Mart** | Very Low | Glass windows, central location. | Death trap; stay away at all costs. |

When navigating between these points, stick to the edges of the map. The center of the map, particularly around the shopping store or "Mart," is an open killing field where giants can spot you from across the terrain.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tower Scouting">
Climb the tower near the graveyard at the start of the round. This allows you to see exactly where the giant spawns without being in immediate danger. Once you identify the spawn, move in the opposite direction toward the cabin or cave.
</Callout>

Sound is equally important. While the game does not currently feature a complex noise-making system for players, the giant's audio is your primary radar. The "Locust" dialogue and the heavy thudding of feet are directional. If the audio is getting louder, do not run; running increases your visual profile. Instead, find a bush and remain perfectly still.
