<Section icon={Swords} title="Evading Roaming Giants">

If a giant spots you, stealth is no longer an option. You must transition into active evasion. Giants move faster than your standard walking speed, meaning straight-line running will result in getting caught.

To successfully evade a giant, you must utilize the environment to break their tracking. Sharp turns around large boulders, dropping down cliffs, and weaving through tight tree clusters will force the monster's AI to recalculate its path, giving you precious seconds to find a new hiding spot.

| Evasion Maneuver | Execution | Success Rate | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cliff Drop** | Jump off a high ledge to the ground below | High | Medium (Fall damage potential) |
| **Boulder Looping** | Run in tight circles around a massive rock | Medium | High (Requires precise camera control) |
| **Tree Weaving** | Sprint through dense forest patches diagonally | High | Low (High visual cover) |
